
Altaba Inc. (formerly Yahoo!) announced a pre-dissolution liquidating distribution of $51.50 in cash per share on September 6, 2019, which was paid on September 23, 2019. This initial payout was part of the company's plan to liquidate and dissolve, with the amount being slightly lower than earlier, higher estimates.

Key Details on the Initial Distribution:
- Amount: $51.50 per share.
- Announcement Date: September 6, 2019.
- Payment Date: September 23, 2019.
- Record Date: September 16, 2019.
- Context: This was the first major cash distribution following the approval of the liquidation plan to return value to stockholders, primarily derived from its large holdings in Alibaba Group Holding Ltd..
